HKSAR v Lai Chee Ying

A major pre-trial ruling on designated judges, public statements, overseas counsel, and alleged abuse of process.

Case law Case 2023-05-29

Procedural posture

Application for permanent stay of proceedings in Jimmy Lai's criminal trial.

Issues

  • abuse of process
  • apparent bias
  • designated judges
  • overseas counsel
  • fair trial

Outcome

The Court of First Instance dismissed the stay application.
