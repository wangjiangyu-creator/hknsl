HKSAR v Chung Hon Lam

Early secession sentencing decision involving Studentlocalism, online platforms, overseas advocacy, and fundraising links.

Case law Case 2021-11-23

Procedural posture

Sentencing after guilty plea to secession and money laundering charges.

Issues

  • secession
  • active participant
  • online advocacy
  • fundraising

Outcome

The District Court sentenced the defendant after treating him as an active participant in secession under Article 20.
