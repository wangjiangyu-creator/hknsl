HKSAR v Ma Chun Man

Leading Court of Appeal decision on classifying incitement to secession as serious or minor and on how local sentencing principles operate within the NSL framework.

Case law Case 2022-08-03

Procedural posture

Application for leave to appeal sentence for incitement to secession.

Issues

  • incitement to secession
  • serious nature
  • sentencing principles
  • online and public speech

Outcome

Leave was granted, the appeal was treated as the appeal proper, and the sentence was reduced to five years' imprisonment.

Comparative note

Compare this case with common-law treatment of national security evidence, jury limits, executive certificates, and fair-trial safeguards.
