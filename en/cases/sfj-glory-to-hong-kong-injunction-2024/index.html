Secretary for Justice v Persons Conducting Prohibited Acts Concerning a Song

Important digital-speech and remedies case on civil injunctions supporting national security criminal law and online takedown dynamics.

Case law Case 2024-05-08

Procedural posture

Appeal from refusal of an interlocutory injunction in aid of criminal law concerning 'Glory to Hong Kong'.

Issues

  • injunction
  • online platforms
  • Chief Executive certificate
  • national anthem
  • sedition

Outcome

The Court of Appeal allowed the appeal and granted an interim injunction restraining specified criminal acts connected with the song.

Comparative note

Compare this case with common-law treatment of national security evidence, jury limits, executive certificates, and fair-trial safeguards.
